Polish Heritage: Quiz Explorer

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Privacy Policy Terms of Use

1. Introduction

Welcome to Polish Heritage: Quiz Explorer ("the App"). This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

The App is developed by Oleg Tyshchenko ("we", "us", or "our") as a free educational application to help users learn about Polish history, culture, cuisine, music, and traditions through interactive content and quizzes.

2. Information We Collect

Our App is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and store only minimal information necessary for the App to function properly. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Local Preferences We Store:

  • Theme Preference: Your choice of light mode, dark mode, or system default appearance.
  • Language Preference: Your selected language (English or Polish).
  • Menu Seed: A random number used to generate personalized traditional menu suggestions for the Wigilia (Christmas Eve) feature.

These preferences are stored solely on your device using standard mobile storage mechanisms and are never uploaded or shared.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We want to be completely transparent: our App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Quiz scores, learning progress, or performance data
  • Browsing history or app usage patterns
  • Photos, contacts, or other personal files
  • Any data for advertising purposes

The App contains no analytics tools, no tracking SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no user account system. Your usage of the App is completely anonymous.

4. Third-Party Services

The App includes a feature that allows you to view locations of Polish natural wonders on a map. When you choose to use this feature by tapping "View in Maps," the App will open Google Maps (or your device's default maps application) with the geographic coordinates of the selected location.

This action is entirely user-initiated and only occurs when you explicitly tap the maps button. The only data shared with the maps service is the geographic coordinates of the point of interest you selected. No personal data is shared.

We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy if you use this feature: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

All App data is stored locally on your device using standard mobile platform storage mechanisms. Since we do not collect or transmit personal data to any servers, there is no risk of data breaches affecting your personal information through our services.

Your locally stored preferences (theme, language, menu seed) can be cleared at any time by:

  • Clearing the App's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

6. Children's Privacy

Polish Heritage: Quiz Explorer is an educational app suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Since our App does not collect personal data, create user accounts, or include any social features, it can be safely used by children. Parents and guardians can feel confident that their children's privacy is protected while using our App.

7. Offline Functionality

Polish Heritage: Quiz Explorer is designed as an offline-first application. All educational content, quizzes, and features are bundled with the App and do not require an internet connection to function. The only feature that uses the internet is the optional "View in Maps" functionality described above.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Continued use of the App after any modifications to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, traditional data subject rights (access, correction, deletion, etc.) do not apply in the conventional sense. However, you always have full control over the minimal local data stored on your device and can delete it at any time through your device settings.
